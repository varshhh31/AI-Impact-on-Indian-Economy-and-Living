# Project1
AI Impact on Indian Economy and Living
Domain
Data Analytics | Business Intelligence | Artificial Intelligence.
Project overview:
    Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming industries by improving productivity, automating tasks, reducing costs, and enhancing decision-making. This project aims to analyze the impact of AI adoption across various sectors in India and evaluate its influence on economic growth, employment, productivity, and living standards.
The study will combine government datasets, industry reports, and survey data to identify trends and provide meaningful insights through interactive Power BI dashboards.
Problem Statement
As AI adoption increases across industries, its effects on employment, productivity, revenue growth, and quality of life need to be measured and analyzed. Organizations and policymakers require data-driven insights to understand how AI contributes to economic development and societal progress.
Objectives
Analyze AI adoption across different sectors in India.
Measure the impact of AI on employment and productivity.
Evaluate revenue growth and cost reduction resulting from AI implementation.
Study the influence of AI on healthcare, education, and overall living standards.
Create an interactive Power BI dashboard for data visualization and decision-making.

Negative Impacts

AI Impact on Living (Lifestyle)
Positive Impacts
Personalized and convenient daily experiences through voice assistants and recommendations.
Improved healthcare and education with smart diagnosis and online learning platforms.
Easier navigation, digital payments, and enhanced fraud detection.
Negative Impacts
Privacy and data security concerns.
Over-dependence on technology in daily life.
Increased misinformation and reduced human interaction in some services.
